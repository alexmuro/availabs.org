Public APIs

At AVAIL we are vociferous consumers of Open Data and we love using API's. We ceate API's to extend Open Data & make it easier to use.

Geo GTFS API

Based on the GTFS Data Exchange, this API allows direct access to GTFS feeds for hundreds of transit agencies. It extends GTFS by adding route & stop geography (GoeJSON & TopoJSON), as well as a number of analytic data (ex route headway, stop wait times).

Built on top of our Geo GTFS API, GTFS Analyst is a visualization and informatics tool for transit agencies.

WimWEB


WimWEB is a visualization platform for Weigh in Motion data & Classification count data from automatic traffic recorders (ATR). It gives spatial context to ATR data and creates a wide array visulizations, quality analysis and standard reports. The data available in this demo is from New York DOT from 2004 to 2011.

About

AVAIL (Albany Visualization and Informatics Lab) is part of the Lewis Mumford Center for Demographic Analysis at the University of Albany, State University of New York. We are a data science lab specializing in transportation, demographic and economic data with an emphasis on web based solutions & spatial visualizations. We are Open Source, Open Data & Open Government advocates.
